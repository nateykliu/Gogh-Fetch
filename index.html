What is Hot Pot?

Hot pot or hotpot, also known as shabu shabu or steamboat, is a dish of Chinese origin whereby a heat source placed on the dining table keeps a pot of soup stock simmering, and a variety of meats and other ingredients are served beside the pot for the diners to put into the hot stock. It originated during the "Three Kingdoms" period (200 - 280AD).
